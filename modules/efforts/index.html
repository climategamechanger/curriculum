Module: Current efforts to address climate change

Local and global initiatives to understand and respond to climate change

Learning Outcomes

Understand current efforts to understand and respond to climate change

  • (Example: You are aware of reputable, evidence-based initiatives to understand and address climate change.)
  • (Example: You know how to distinguish between reputable and non-reputable organizations and initiatives.)

Readings

IPCC: The Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change

The home page for the United Nations body for assessing the science related to climate change.

30 minutes

Hawaii Climate Change Portal

Information on efforts Hawaii is taking to address climate change, including adaptation, carbon sequestration, tracking greenhouse gas emissions, setting energy policy, and sustainability plans.

30 minutes

City and County of Honolulu's Office of Climate Change, Sustainability, and Resiliency

Oahu’s response to climate change, focusing on four discovery areas: remaining rooted, bouncing forward, island innovation and exposure, and laulima.

15 minutes

UH students plant 1000 trees in a day

To cut down on emissions, UH students and volunteers plant 1,000 trees in a single day.

11 minutes

Gov Ige Enacts Bills Rooted in the Spirit of Mālama Honua

The bills resonate with continued efforts of the Polynesian Voyaging Society and legacy of Hōkūleʻa to inspire and grow a movement to mālama honua, to care for our Island Earth.

11 minutes

The Nature Conservancy's 10 Groups to Watch in 2019

Saving the planet must be an all-hands-on-deck effort. In no particular order, we present 10 groups to watch in 2019.

10 minutes

Climate Game Changers

A UK site overviewing several approaches to climate change, including iron salt aerosol, carbon pricing, nuclear power, grassroots NGOs, and more.
