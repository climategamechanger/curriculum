Modules Topics covered in this class.

This page presents the “modules”, or the topics that are covered in this course.

Click on the tile associated with a module to go to a page containing that module’s contents.

The current module structure is just an example. There are many ways to organize the material. Please feel free to suggest alternative organizations!

1. Introduction

Overview of the Climate Game Changer course, and some simple initial tasks to get started.

2. Climate change in a nutshell

A broad overview of issues in climate change.

3. Climate change and land

How climate change is predicted to impact on land masses.

4. Climate change and water

How climate change is predicted to impact on oceans.

5. Climate change and Hawaii

How climate change is predicted to impact on Hawaii.

6. Economic implications of climate change

The economic impacts of fighting climate change, as well as the economic results of climate change.

7. Ethical implications of climate change

The ethics of climate change, including the legal status of climate refugees.

8. Current efforts to address climate change

Local and global initiatives to understand and respond to climate change

9. What you can do now

Take a personal action to help address climate change
